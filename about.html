Anderson Lot

I am an Electrical Engineer who recently discovered a passion for programming.

My first contact with programming was through the classic C language. Later, I got to know Arduino and developed an interest in electronics and microcontrollers.

I started some projects using Processing, which utilizes a framework very similar to Arduino's, based on JavaScript. At that time, I developed software for calculating quantities in distribution networks, but I didn't share the project since I didn't yet know GitHub or other communities. Soon, I intend to showcase and improve this software here along with my other projects.

More recently, I discovered the P5.js framework, which made it much easier to build small projects to stay connected to this universe during the little free time I had in recent years. And now I've discovered GitHub and am in love with the platform, eager to develop projects in the community.

In this portfolio, which is also a work in progress, you will find some of the projects I have completed or am still working on. You can follow all the repositories of these projects on my GitHub profile. Feel free to contribute or give suggestions there.

In the future, I plan to unify my WordPress blog project here on this same site. For now, you can check out the content on WordPress!
